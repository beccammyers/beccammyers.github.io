About Me

My name is Rebecca Myers, and I am currently a student at Brigham Young University-Idaho studying Early Childhood/Special Education. I have had a passion for working with young children since I started babysitting as a young teenager and as I taught swimming lessons. The joy that comes from working with young children followed me through to my first time teaching in a first-grade elementary class. As I’ve taken classes and had opportunities to work with children, my educational philosophy has grown and developed. I have discovered that students learn best when the activities and lessons are tailored to the individual and are developmentally appropriate for their age. I’m excited to design a learning environment where children participate in hands-on activities and are eager to participate and learn.
